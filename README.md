# College OLX Android

College OLX is an Android-based mobile application designed for students and teachers to easily buy, sell, or exchange academic items such as books, study materials, and instruments. The app provides a convenient platform for trading both free and paid items within the campus community, promoting resource sharing and affordability in education.
